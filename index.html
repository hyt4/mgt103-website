About This Course

MGT 103 examines how businesses operate within the broader social, ethical, and economic landscape. Students explore real-world case studies, leadership principles, and the responsibilities that come with building and managing organizations.

Through hands-on projects and collaborative discussions, this course prepares students to think critically about decision-making, stakeholder impact, and sustainable business practices in a rapidly changing world.

Whether you are a future entrepreneur, manager, or changemaker — this course gives you the tools to lead with purpose and clarity.
